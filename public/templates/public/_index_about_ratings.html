What Are Cyber Security & Digital Trust Ratings?

Ratings are compiled from non-intrusive scans, open source intelligence and externally available data. They provide an outside-in or "hacker's" eye view of an organisation. They quantify the public security posture of any organisation with an online presence.


We evauluate the implementation of a number of crucial open security standards that ensures the integrity, authenticity and confidentiality of internet delivered data and communications.


When woven together compliance to these standards increases security and trust on the Internet: users can reliably ascertain where they are going, who they are interacting with, and how confidential their interactions are.


This is combined with open source intelligence and data from numerous security intelligence feeds, intrusion detection feeds and a system of automated bots, crawlers and honeypots which continuously collect data from across the Internet.

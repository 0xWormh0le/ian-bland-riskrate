What does a cyber security consultant do?

A security consultant is someone who is aware of every aspect of cybersecurity. Someone who works as a guide, an advisor, and an experienced practitioner.

Just as important as their technical skills and knowledge, you need someone who understands the needs of a small business not someone who is trying to bring enterprise level cybersecurity thinking or solutions to a small business environment.

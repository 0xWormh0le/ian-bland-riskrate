Industry Uses

Cyber Security & Digital Trust bulk assessments.

Vendor Risk Management

Some of the most devastating breaches in the past few years have been rooted in the security weaknesses of third parties, and in fact, hackers themselves admit that contractors are often their primary target.

Ratings allow companies to quickly and cost effectively analyse and monitor the security posture of multiple vendors and partners to reduce their own risk profile and enhance compliance.

Based on verifiable data they are an improvement on, or add tremendous value to, self report security questionnaires currently used when assessing third party risk.

They allow an organisation to get an immediate profile of a third party, improving the time and reducing the resources involved in doing so.

Additionally an enterprise account allows an organisation to provide security resources to third parties to help them achieve security compliance.

Many smaller businesses that are part of a larger supply chain don't have the knowledge or resources to implement cyber security initiatives. We provide everything a business might need to meet enterprise level compliance, risk management and IT assurance programmes.

An easy to follow Cyber Security Action Plan saves time, saves money and quickly plugs security gaps in your digital eco-sphere.

Cyber Insurance Providers

Long reliant on self report questionnaires insurance providers can now assess the risk of prospective customers using tangible, transparent and verifiable data. Data that provides an insight to risk from some of the most common and easily executed cyber crimes.

Insurers can monitor a company's rating for the duration of a policy to ensure compliance to security standards and flag any vulnerabilities or malware infections.

Not only does this improve cyber risk assessment it allows an insurer to provide remediation to a customer and access to cyber security resources. This improves a customer's security profile, reducing the chance of a policy claim.

Security Research - Institutional Use

With the ability to cost effectively scan thousands of domains, Government Agencies and institutional Security Researchers can analyse organisations, industries and government assets on a large-scale.

Reports and remediation provide data that is inline with the requirements from:

Binding Operational Directive 18-01 - Enhance Email and Web Security
Department of Homeland Security, USA

Government technology standards and guidance
Government Digital Service, UK

Remediation Reports

Making security, clear, straightforward and effective for organisations of all sizes.

A full Cyber Security & Digital Trust Report will give you an easy to understand breakdown of your rating and how it can impact your organisation. Full remediation recommendations are provided, allowing you to immediately rectify any vulnerabilities.

Enterprise Plan customers can provide remediation reports to their third parties enhancing the security of their digital ecosystem.

Remediation recommendations are broken down into easy to follow categories and backed with a library of cyber security resources to make them actionable for even small organisations with limited IT resources.

Report Overview

At first glance our reports provides a clear easy to understand general overview of a rating. You can immediately see your rating and risk profile. Any category of your digital presence in which you might be at risk is clearly identified.

You can then drill down into each of the main categories for a full explanation of the impact they might have on your organisation and from here view each of the sub-categories in a simple to use, clear and logical interface.

Expandable sub-categry sections provide data on each of the test performed and the associated results. Quickly and efficiently you can identify any organisational weak spots and access remediation recommendations.

Consolidated View

The consolidated view of the Enterprise Plan allows you to view the results of each tracked organisation. An average overall rating, average scores for each category and rating distribution are provided.

You can view all of your tracked organisations or subsets of them based on sectors, system categories or your own custom tags. In each view you can export results (CSV, Excel & PDF) and filter organisations according to their overall and category ratings.

Quickly and clearly you can identify organisations based on their risk profile. In each instance you have access to the full report view of an organisation.

Report Data View

The data view provides the complete data set of the rating findings for a specific organisation. These can be exported in a number of formats, CSV, Excel, PDF or simply printed.

Each data category item links to the relevant section in our resource library to give you a full understanding of the relevance of the data and how to ensure compliance for each standard.

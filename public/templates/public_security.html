Response Readiness

Respond & Recover from security incidents
Incident Response is a crucial part of the cyber security framework. Ensuring that you have the processes and structure in place to deal with any security incident will enable you to mitigate damages and recover quickly. Security incidents are unavoidable. Preparations must be in place to quickly identify and respond to an incident. Whether it is a lost laptop or a malicious insider, organizations must be ready to respond. Having a plan available ahead of time is paramount to successfully managing the situation.

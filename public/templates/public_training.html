Cyber Security Training

Despite sophisticated IT security defenses, one weak link – employees – remains a major vulnerability for organisations of all sizes. Providing your staff with regular awareness training on key issues is essential to fully protect your organisation. We have taken the hassle out of cyber security training, simply sign up and we'll do the rest. Each week your staff will receive quality training in a critical area of cyber security, followed by a test of their knowledge. All you need to do is sit back and wait for the weekly reports. That's all there is to it!!

1. Add Your Users

Upload a csv file containing email addresses and names (to personalise the training). Then choose a date on which to start your training. No subscription or recurring monthly fees required, a simple one-off payment ensures delivery of training to all of your staff.

2. Training is Sent

Each week your users receive a link to a training course via email. They simply click the link, then complete the training and quiz. No logins are required. Automatic reminder emails are sent to those users that have not completed scheduled training.

3. Receive Reports

Detailed weekly reports on the training completion status are sent right to your inbox. Need immediate access to your data? Your admin area is available 24/7 for full access to all statistics. We also provide a complete LMS system for advanced training delivery.
